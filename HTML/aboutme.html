About Me

Welcome to my life!

Hi there!

My name is Thomas Baines and I am 23 years old. I was born and raised in England, in a lovely city called Sheffield. I have a fantastic family who all currently live back in the UK. They are awesome and a great support to me whilst I am living in the USA!

I am studying Finance which I would like to use in order to live elsewhere in the USA. At the moment New York, Washington DC and Austin all excite me although I have never been to any of them. I have a start-up cleaning business back in the UK which I still enjoy being involved in, who knows, maybe one day it will let me retire early. Connect with me on LinkedIn!
